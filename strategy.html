Crazy 4 Poker

Until extremely recently, the general rule here was to fold if you didn't have a K+Q+8+4 or better

Using mathematics, I determined that you should never fold on a K+Q+7+6+5, and that you should generally not fold on a K+Q+8+4+2

We are extremely close to verifying the exact optimal strategy for this game finally, which is very exciting!

Four Card Poker

The simple strategy is to raise x3 with a pair of tens or better, raise x1 with a pair of twos or better, and to fold otherwise.

The Wizard of Odds website has a more advanced strategy which gets within 0.1% of the optimal house edge.

I have heard rumors that gambling expert James Grosjean has developed an even more advanced strategy.

Heads Up Hold'Em

Raise x4 if you have an Ace, if you have a King with 5-Queen, if you have a Queen with a 8-Jack, or a Jack with a 10.

Additionally raise x4 if you have a suited King with anything, a Queen with 6 or 7, or a Jack with 8 or 9.

Texas Hold'Em Bonus

If you need a straight or better to win on the Ante, fold an 2 with a 3-7 if they have different suits.

If you need a flush or better to win on the Ante, fold an 2 with a 3-7 if they have different suits, or an unsuited 3+4.
